Canadian River

The Canadian River is a tributary of the Arkansas River, approximately 906 miles (1,458 km) long, in the central United States. It drains an area of 45,000 square miles (120,000 km2) in northeastern New Mexico, north ern and eastern Oklahoma, and northwestern Arkansas.

Jog Falls
Nature :)

Jog Falls is a waterfall located in the state of Karnataka, India. It is the second-highest plunge waterfall in India, with a total height of 253 m (830 ft). It is a segmented waterfall created by the Sharavathi River falling from a height of 253 metres (830 ft).
